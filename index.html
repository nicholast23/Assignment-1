Tiffany Day

Biography

Tiffany Day was born in Toronto, Canada on January 30, 2000, but grew up in Whichita, Kansas and is currently based in Los Angeles, California. Day is a singer-songwriter who grew up classically trained in piano and violin and self taught on the guitar. She rose to fame after posting song covers on her YouTube channel in late 2016. She released her first original song “Bubble” on August 16, of 2018.
